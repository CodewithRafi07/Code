KHAN'S FILMOGRAPHY is a cinematography and filmmaking studio built by KHAN SIAM on the belief that light, shadow, and motion can express what words cannot.

I love creating visual content, editing videos, and exploring filmmaking. I focus on impactful history detailing and visual quality. Currently, I work as a marketing head at “Adjective Bangladesh,” combining creativity with branding and strategy.
